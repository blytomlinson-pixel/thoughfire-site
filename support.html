Common Topics

Voice Recording Issues

Ensure microphone permissions are enabled for Thought Fire in your device settings.

Motion and Orientation Features

Relax-to-Wake and other sensor-based features may require motion permissions depending on your device.

Lost Notes

Thought Fire stores notes locally on your device. Deleting the app may remove stored content.
