Thought Fire

Beta Testing

Thank you for your interest in helping test Thought Fire. Thought Fire is designed to help you capture ideas, insights, and inspiration that occur during the transition between wakefulness and sleep before they fade away.

📱 Android Users

Thought Fire is currently available through Google Play Internal Testing.

Important: Google requires Android beta testers to be added to the testing list before installation.

To participate:

  1. Send the Gmail address associated with your Android phone to rob@thoughtfire.app
  2. Wait for confirmation that you've been added to the tester list.
  3. Use the Android Beta Link below.
  4. Install Thought Fire through Google Play.
Android Beta Link

🍎 iPhone Users

Thought Fire is currently available through Apple's TestFlight program.

To participate:

  1. Install Apple's free TestFlight app.
  2. Open the TestFlight invitation link.
  3. Accept the invitation.
  4. Install Thought Fire.
iPhone Beta Link

Note: If TestFlight isn't already installed, Apple will guide you through installing it first.

Feedback

Please report:

  • Bugs or crashes
  • Confusing screens or instructions
  • Feature suggestions
  • General impressions
  • Successful captures and experiences

Your feedback will directly influence future versions of Thought Fire.

Thank you for helping bring this project to life!
