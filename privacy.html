Thought Fire Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Thought Fire is designed to help users capture ideas and insights during the transition between wakefulness and sleep.

Information We Collect

Thought Fire does not require users to create an account.

The app may store user-created content such as text notes, voice recordings, app preferences, and settings.

How Information Is Used

Notes, recordings, and settings are used solely to provide the functionality of the app.

Data Storage

User-created content is stored locally on the user's device. Thought Fire does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Sensors and Device Features

Thought Fire may use touch input, motion and orientation sensors, and audio recording capabilities. These features are used only to provide app functionality.

Third-Party Services

Thought Fire may use platform services provided by Apple or Google for app distribution and analytics.

Children's Privacy

Thought Fire is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Contact

Questions may be directed to: rob@thoughtfire.app
